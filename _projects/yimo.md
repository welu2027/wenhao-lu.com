**[YIMO](https://www.yimo-official.org/)** is the **Youth International Math Olympiad**: a free, international math competition open to middle and high school students from every corner of the world. I founded and co-direct YIMO to give young mathematicians a rigorous, high-quality platform to challenge themselves and compete globally.

---

## Motivation

Many talented students, especially those from **underrepresented or resource-limited backgrounds**, never get to compete in top-tier international math competitions. The barriers are real: cost, access, prior training, and connections. YIMO was built to eliminate them. **An open competition** with no entry fees, prior experience, or gatekeeping required. Over **95% of our registrants have zero prior competition experience** -- and that is exactly the point. YIMO is built for first-timers and veterans alike, from every country and background.
