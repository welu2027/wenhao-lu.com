**[DSH Hacks](https://www.dshhacks.org/)** is a global student hackathon challenging participants to build meaningful AI-powered tools for STEM education. View the inaugural event on [Devpost](https://dsh-hacks-v1.devpost.com/).

---

## The Challenge

**Theme: AI x STEM Education.** Participants design and build a technical product that uses AI to improve, expand, or make STEM education more accessible. Projects have ranged from ML-powered personalized learning tools to computer vision simulations for labs to accessibility platforms for underrepresented communities. Judged on creativity, feasibility, technical depth, and real-world impact. Open to students **13+ from all countries**, and no prior experience is required.

This event is a direct extension of NEXT Horizon's mission: a **STEM talent accelerator** dedicated to equipping students left behind in STEM with the skills, mentorship, and real-world experience to break into the field.
