## AI Safety & Interpretability — New York University

**AI Safety Researcher** | 2026 – present

I am building the first interpretability and observability library for World Models and State Space Models (SSMs), analogous to [TransformerLens](https://github.com/TransformerLensOrg/TransformerLens) but designed for WM architectures including IRIS, DreamerV3, and V-JEPA. I am the only high schooler in a research group of NYU graduate students.

I am also developing a Neuronpedia-style interactive visualization platform to allow researchers to inspect world model internals in real time.

**Spectral Graph Theory Researcher** | 2025 – present
